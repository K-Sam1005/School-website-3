Campus Life

Computer Lab

The age of technology is upon us and it is becoming increasingly difficult for someone to function without a knowledge of computers. At Podar International School, we realize this and have a state-of-the-art computer lab in the school premises. The computer lab is air-conditioned with the adequate number of latest PCs which have all the required software installed. All PCs have a high-speed internet connection. The lab is specially designed to educate the students with the latest technology so that the children get comfortable with technology at a very young age.

School Library

There are well-equipped Science Laboratories, Maths Laboratory and Computer Hub for teachers. The school also has a well-stocked library with books and periodicals.

School Play Ground

Our school has a state of the art playground where New Age, as well as traditional sports, are played. We have sports for all age groups so that children can relax and exercise at the same time with a sporting spirit. The school has appointed a trained Physical Training Master to train the children in various indoor games like chess and carrom and outdoor games like football, volleyball, basketball etc. We encourage students to participate in all sports events.
